Borscht recipe

Ingredients:

  • 3 medium beets
  • 3 carrots
  • 3 medium baking potatoes
  • 1/2 medium cabbage
  • 1/2 kg of beef stew meat
  • etc.

Directions:

  1. Heat the pot. Add the stew beef.
    Let the beef brown lightly on one side, then turn over.
  2. Pour 4 cups of the beef broth over the beef in the pot.
    Cover and cook until the meat is ready.
  3. Meanwhile chop the veggies.
  4. etc.
